About us

Services

Jigs and Fixtures

We design, manufacture and deliver jigs and fixtures to a range of customers in many industries, such as aerospace, automotive and many more. Our products are made to suit all of the metal finishing processes such as: anodising, painitng, zincing and many more.

CNC Machining

We specialise in CNC maching, and provide quality turned and milled parts to customers specifications. We have a range of machinery and experienced staff suited to provided the best service on the market.

Welding and Fabrications

We specialise in Mig and Tig welding. Providing a range of services on all metals from aluminium, mild steel, titanium, stainless steel and many more. We are available to work on site for projects big and small and manufacture a range of racking and shelving in house for all sorts of bespoke storage solutions.
